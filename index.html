About Me

Yuelu graduated from Yonsei University in South Korea with a major in Media Communication. Currently, she is a MPhil in Computational Media and Arts at HKUST(GZ). Her research focuses primarily on interactive narrative content creation under the intervention of AI agents. Her research area is Human-centered technology for storytelling including interactive storytelling, transmedia storytelling, and AIGC.
