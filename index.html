About Me

I am an MPhil student in Computational Media and Arts at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), where I am a member of the Creative Art Tech (CAT) team. I am supervised by Professor David Kei Man Yip and co-supervised by Professor Luwen Yu.

My research focuses on human-centered technology for storytelling, with a particular emphasis on interactive storytelling, transmedia storytelling, and AI-generated content (AIGC). I am interested in how AI agents can be integrated into the storytelling process to assist creators in building narratives that engage audiences while maintaining creative control.

I graduated from Yonsei University in South Korea with a Bachelor’s degree in Media Communication. Throughout my academic career, I have been passionate about exploring the intersection of technology and narrative, particularly how emerging technologies like AI can transform the ways stories are told across various platforms.
